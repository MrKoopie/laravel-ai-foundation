# Visual Review: Laravel Spark Alpha

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LFY_P-jnuXM
Reviewed: 2026-06-29

## Observations

- Silent product walkthrough of Laravel Spark alpha.
- Shows public marketing/pricing pages.
- Shows onboarding after signup.
- Shows settings screens for profile, password/security, two-factor authentication, subscription, invoices, and teams.
- Briefly shows configuration/code related to plan/team setup.

## Programming Value

The clip has limited direct coding value, but it is useful as a checklist for evaluating generated SaaS scaffolding: registration, settings, security, billing, invoices, and teams should all be exercised before treating a starter kit as production-ready.
